# Idea Reviewer - Scoring Criteria

## Five Evaluation Dimensions

### 1. Strategic Fit

**What to assess:**
- Does this align with the Rithum for Brands platform direction?
- Does it support known OKRs, roadmap themes, or executive priorities?
- Is this a "core" enhancement or a "nice to have"?
- Does it strengthen a competitive differentiator or address a known gap?

**Ratings:**
- **Strong** - directly supports stated strategy or OKRs with clear evidence
- **Moderate** - adjacent to strategy; could be justified with the right framing
- **Weak** - tangential; hard to connect to stated priorities
- **Unknown** - insufficient context to assess

### 2. Customer Value

**What to assess:**
- What customer segment benefits, and how broadly?
- Is there evidence of demand (tickets, sales feedback, churn risk, NPS signal)?
- What is the customer's current workaround, if any?
- Is this a pain point or a nice-to-have?

**Ratings:** Strong / Moderate / Weak / Unknown (same scale as above)

### 3. Feasibility

**What to assess:**
- Does this fit within existing architecture and systems?
- Are there known dependencies or blockers?
- Is this a net-new capability or an enhancement of something existing?
- Are there compliance, security, or integration requirements that add complexity?

**Ratings:** Strong / Moderate / Weak / Unknown

### 4. Effort Signal

**What to assess:**
- Based on available information, is this a small, medium, or large effort?
- Are there integrations, compliance requirements, or third-party dependencies that inflate complexity?
- Does this require design, legal review, or external approvals?

**Flag:** If effort cannot be estimated without engineering input, say so explicitly.

### 5. Risk / Concerns

**What to assess:**
- What could go wrong?
- Are there competing initiatives that overlap?
- Is there a risk of scope creep if approved?
- Are there dependencies on teams or systems outside this PM's control?
- Is there a timing risk (regulatory, competitive, seasonal)?

## Recommendation Options

| Recommendation | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Pursue** | Strong signal across most dimensions. Move to business case or PRD. |
| **Investigate Further** | Promising but one or more dimensions are Unknown or Weak. Specify exactly what needs to be answered. |
| **Defer** | Idea has merit but timing, capacity, or strategic fit don't support it now. Revisit in [timeframe]. |
| **Decline** | Weak strategic fit, low customer value, or risks outweigh benefits. Do not invest further. |
